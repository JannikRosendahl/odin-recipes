How to Make Wok with Noodles

Quick and easy recipe, you can replace tofu with chicken if you like

Ingredients

  • Scallions
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Bell Pepper
  • Soy Sauce, Peanut Oil
  • Noodles
  • Tofu
  • Carrot
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Fry tofu first
  2. Add onions and garlic
  3. Add bell pepper and carrot
  4. Cook noodles and broccoli
  5. Add noodles and broccoli to pan
  6. Add spices, then soy sauce, then peanut oil
  7. Serve
